ABOUT US

Our mission is to redefine what it means to be a marine classification society in the 21st century so that we can help modernize the maritime industry.
We believe that it is our obligation to facilitate safer shipping, cleaner seas, and improved standards for marine safety and environment protection. That’s why we’ve made it a priority to help make state-of-the-art technology accessible, on a global scale, to the entire maritime world.
Furthermore, our goal is always to be a customer service business first, that just happens to be in the marine classification industry. That’s why we’re committed to continuously updating our services to ensure that we can provide maximum value, convenience, and service. As an Independent body engaged in inspection and certification, CRS also act as recognized organization for flag state administrations to certify vessels flying their flag according to various national and international regulations.
We also perform certification of management systems of companies in accordance to International Safety Management code as well as various ISO management system standards.
Our expertise in the maritime regulatory sphere make us right choice to develop compliance solutions for your organization and your fleet in an ever changing regulatory regime. Our technical expertise in marine technology and associated disciplines can be tapped in to for engineering you next asset or modifying your existing asset to meet new requirements.
